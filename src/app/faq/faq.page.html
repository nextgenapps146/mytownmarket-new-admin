About us
My Town Market is a service that encourages its users to shop in store by presenting them with some irresistable and amazing deals online. This platform is a hyper local marketplace where local shops can create their ads which they can choose to either run in their local area or other areas. We have deals in a lot of different categores some of which include Groceries, Meat, Food, Clothing. My Town Market also lists deals in the service industry like Appliance Repair, Plumbers, Carpenters, AC Service & Repair, Salon for Men & Women, etc.
